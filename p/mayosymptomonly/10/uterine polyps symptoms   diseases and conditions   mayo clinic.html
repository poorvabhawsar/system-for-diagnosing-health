Uterine polyps

Symptoms

By Mayo Clinic Staff

Signs of uterine polyps include:

  • Irregular menstrual bleeding â€” for example, having frequent, unpredictable periods of variable length and heaviness
  • Bleeding between menstrual periods
  • Excessively heavy menstrual periods
  • Vaginal bleeding after menopause
  • Infertility

Some women may experience only light bleeding or spotting or may even be symptom-free.

When to see a doctor

Seek medical care if you have:

  • Vaginal bleeding after menopause
  • Bleeding between menstrual periods
  • Irregular menstrual bleeding
