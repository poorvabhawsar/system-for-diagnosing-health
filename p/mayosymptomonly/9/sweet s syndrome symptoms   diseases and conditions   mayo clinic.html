  • Sweet's syndrome

Sweet's syndrome is marked by an abrupt eruption of small red bumps on your arms, neck, face or back â€” often after a fever or upper respiratory infection. The bumps grow quickly in size, spreading into painful clusters up to an inch or so in diameter.

When to see a doctor

If you develop a painful, red rash that quickly grows in size, see your doctor for appropriate treatment.
